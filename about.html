How to play:

Move your fox with the right arrow or left arrow of your keyboard.

The goal is to reach the highest possible point by jumping on the various platforms: the higher you go, the harder it becomes. Keep in mind that some platforms will allow you to jump higher.
Beware! Traps will try to kill you and some platforms will disappear after you have jumped on them!

About us:

This game is created for the course of Interactive Graphics 2020/2021 of "Sapienza" University by Andrea Frabotta and Flavio Scaccia.

Libraries:

Three.js
Physijs.js - Used to perform collisions
Tween.js - Used to create animations of the fox and of the trap
GLTFLoader.js - Used to load the hierarchical model of the fox
